ABOUT ME

I was born on October 23, 1971, in the area of Janzour-Al Mashashata, on the outskirts of the capital, Tripoli. In the first six months after birth, I suffered from a fever that led to contracting polio. When I reached the age of six in 1976, I traveled to Belgrade, the capital of Serbia, for treatment. During that time, I also attended primary school at a health center there, learning Serbian. The treatment continued for six continuous years, and I returned to my homeland in 1983.
